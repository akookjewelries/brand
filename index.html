Brand Identity

A Creative
Identity

AKOOK Jewelries is not simply a jewellery brand — it is a creative identity shaped by passion for design, culture, and emotional storytelling. Proudly rooted in Oman, the brand reflects authentic local heritage and craftsmanship while aspiring to earn recognition on the global stage.

The brand exists to transform jewellery from ornament into expression, creating pieces that reflect individuality, memory, and meaning. Every creation is approached as a narrative, carefully shaped through concept, aesthetics, and craftsmanship.

Est. 2020 — Oman

Rooted in artistic exploration and visual storytelling, AKOOK Jewelries embraces the balance between elegance and personality, offering designs that resonate beyond trends and seasons.

"It is jewellery designed not only to be worn, but to be experienced."
KHADIJA

Founder

Khadija
Al Dhabouni

Founder & Creative Director

Since founding AKOOK Jewelries in 2020, Khadija has been dedicated to designing unique and exquisite pieces that reflect Omani culture and craftsmanship. Her role encompasses the creative process from conceptualization to execution, ensuring each design resonates with elegance and authenticity.

She leads a talented team, oversees production, and manages marketing strategies to promote the brand. Her passion for jewelry design drives her to innovate and create pieces that celebrate the heritage of Oman, while also appealing to modern aesthetics.

In addition to being an entrepreneur, Khadija is an industry professional with demonstrable experience in the retail side of the real estate business in the Sultanate of Oman — garnering a wealth of knowledge in planning strategies and delivering high-end campaigns at Majid Al Futtaim malls.

PHILOSOPHY

Design Philosophy

Three Guiding
Principles

Principle 01

Concept Before Decoration

Every design begins with a clearly defined concept. Ornamentation follows meaning — never the other way around. The idea is the architecture; beauty is its expression.

Principle 02

Emotion Embedded in Detail

The smallest decisions carry the greatest weight. A curvature, a texture, a proportion — each detail is a deliberate act of emotional communication.

Principle 03

Balance Between Tradition and Modernity

Inspiration flows from nature, symbolism, architecture, and cultural elements — translated into modern forms that maintain elegance and wearability.

Logo Usage

Logo
Variants

The AKOOK logo must always appear in its approved forms. The icon, wordmark, and sub-brand lockup must maintain their proportional relationships at all times.

On Black

On Forest

On Charcoal

On Light

Incorrect Logo Usage

Proportions

Do not change the size or proportional relationships of the logo elements. The icon and wordmark must always maintain their designed ratio.

Rotation

Do not place the logo on an angle. The logotype must always appear upright and level, never tilted or rotated.

Color Alteration

Do not change the value of the colors. They should not be lightened, darkened, or replaced with unapproved palette colors.

Busy Backgrounds

Do not place the logo on complex, patterned, or photographic backgrounds that compromise legibility or brand recognition.

Effects

Do not add drop shadows, glows, gradients, or any 3D effects to any element of the logo system.

Outline Version

Do not use the logo as an outline-only version. The logotype must appear in its solid, approved weight at all times.
